Hi, welcome to Mathemedic

About
A Maths app for all the lower secondary students to help them better understand the topics taught in secondary 1 and prepare for year 3 and 4.
Purpose
As Secondary 3 students, we find that adapting to secondary school math is a hard thing to do and as a result, the idea for this app had arised. Our aim is to enable our users to have a convenient way of learning math.
Features
  • Interactive Practice Questions and Answers, users can check their inputted answers
  • Concise notes compiled by us, information is what we feel is more important for the users to remember.
